### Raspberry Pi4 bootloader EEPROM release notes
### https://github.com/raspberrypi/rpi-eeprom/blob/master/firmware/release-notes.md

### build the eeprom
rpi-eeprom-config --out pieeprom-customconfig-2023-01-11.bin --config eeprom-config.txt \
   /lib/firmware/raspberrypi/bootloader/stable/pieeprom-2023-01-11.bin

### update the eeprom
sudo rpi-eeprom-update -d -f pieeprom-customconfig-2023-01-11.bin
